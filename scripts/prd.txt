# Living Abroad Budgeting Tool PRD

## Overview
The Living Abroad Budgeting Tool is an interactive web application designed to help families relocating internationally model and plan their financial lives with confidence. Initially focused on supporting moves from the UK to Portugal under the Non-Habitual Resident (NHR) tax regime, the tool aims to provide comprehensive financial planning support for international relocations.

## Core Features

### 1. User Authentication & Profile Management
- Secure user registration and login system
- Profile creation with family composition details
- Save and manage multiple budget scenarios
- Data privacy and GDPR compliance

### 2. Location-Based Configuration
- Country pair selection (source and destination)
- Local tax regime information (focusing initially on UK -> Portugal NHR)
- Cost of living data for both locations
- Currency conversion handling

### 3. Income Planning
- Multiple income source management
- Tax calculation based on selected regimes
- Currency denomination options
- Income timing and frequency settings

### 4. Expense Modeling
- Categorized expense tracking
- Cost of living adjustments
- Housing costs (rent/mortgage)
- Utilities and regular bills
- Education expenses
- Healthcare costs
- Transportation
- Entertainment and leisure
- Emergency fund planning

### 5. Interactive Budget Dashboard
- Visual budget breakdown
- Income vs. expenses comparison
- Savings projections
- Risk analysis
- Monthly cash flow visualization

### 6. Report Generation
- Detailed PDF reports
- Comparative analysis
- Tax implications summary
- Savings recommendations
